Famous Foods of Mysore

Mysore Pak

A traditional sweet made of ghee, sugar, and gram flour.

Bisi Bele Bath

A spicy rice-based dish with lentils, vegetables, and spices.

Masala Dosa

Rice pancake stuffed with spicy potato filling, served with chutney.
